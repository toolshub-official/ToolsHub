About Us

SQL Formatter was created by a team of database professionals and developers who were frustrated with the lack of simple, privacy-focused SQL formatting tools available online.

Our mission is to provide developers with high-quality tools that respect their privacy and work entirely in the browser without requiring any server processing or data collection.

All our tools are:

  • 100% client-side - No data ever leaves your browser
  • Open source - Transparent and community-driven
  • Free forever - No paywalls or premium features
  • Privacy-focused - No tracking, analytics, or data collection

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my SQL code safe when using this formatter?

Absolutely! Our SQL formatter works entirely in your browser. Your code never leaves your device and isn't sent to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your sensitive SQL queries.

Which SQL dialects does this tool support?

The formatter supports standard SQL that works across most database systems including MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server, and SQLite. Some dialect-specific features may not format perfectly.

Can I use this tool without an internet connection?

Yes! After loading the page for the first time, you can use the SQL formatter offline. All processing happens locally in your browser.

How can I contribute to this project?

We welcome contributions! The project is open source and available on GitHub. You can report issues, suggest features, or submit pull requests to help improve the tool.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Here's how we handle your data:

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any SQL code that you input into our formatter. All processing happens entirely in your browser, and your code never leaves your device.

Cookies

We only use essential cookies to remember your preferences (like dark mode setting). We do not use tracking cookies or analytics services.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party services we use are Google AdSense for advertisements. These ads may use cookies for personalized advertising, but they have no access to your SQL code.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
