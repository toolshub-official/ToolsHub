Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PDF compressor tool.

Is it free to compress PDF files?

Yes, our PDF compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can compress as many files as you need without any limitations.

Is my data secure when using this tool?

Absolutely! All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are not uploaded to any server. We don't store or have access to your documents.

What's the maximum file size I can compress?

The tool can handle files up to 100MB in size. For larger files, we recommend splitting them into smaller parts before compression.

Does compression reduce the quality of my PDF?

Our tool uses smart compression algorithms that prioritize maintaining quality while reducing file size. For documents with images, you may notice minimal quality loss at higher compression levels.

What browsers are supported?

The tool works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For best performance, we recommend using the latest version of your browser.
