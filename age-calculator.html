Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

Our calculator is 100% accurate for all dates after 1582 (Gregorian calendar). It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths.

Can I calculate age at a future date?

Yes! Simply enter a future date as the "Age at the Date of" value to see how old you'll be on that date.

Why do months show different lengths?

Our calculator shows actual calendar months, which vary between 28-31 days. This provides the most accurate age calculation.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No, all calculations happen in your browser. We don't store any of your personal data or date information.
