About SnapCraft Pro

SnapCraft Pro is a free online tool that allows anyone to capture high-quality screenshots of websites with ease. Our mission is to provide a simple, fast, and reliable solution for designers, developers, marketers, and anyone who needs website screenshots.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SnapCraft Pro was created to solve the common frustration of capturing full-page website screenshots. Traditional methods often require browser extensions or complex software, but we've simplified the process into a single web-based tool.

How It Works

We use the powerful ScreenshotOne API to generate accurate, high-resolution screenshots of any public website. The process happens entirely in the cloud, so there's no software to install and it works on any device.

Features

  • Full-page website screenshots
  • Mobile and desktop view options
  • No registration required
  • Fast processing times
  • Secure and private
  • Works on all devices

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SnapCraft Pro really free to use?

Yes! SnapCraft Pro is completely free to use with no hidden costs. We may offer premium features in the future, but the basic screenshot functionality will always remain free.

Do you store my screenshots?

No, we don't store your screenshots permanently. The images are generated on-demand and served directly to your browser. Once you close the page, the screenshot is no longer available on our servers.

Can I capture password-protected websites?

No, our tool can only capture publicly accessible websites. Password-protected or private pages cannot be captured for security reasons.

Why is my screenshot not loading?

There could be several reasons: the website might be blocking screenshots, the URL might be incorrect, or the site might be temporarily unavailable. Try checking the URL and trying again.

Can I capture mobile views of websites?

Currently, we capture desktop views by default. We're working on adding mobile view options in a future update.

How can I support SnapCraft Pro?

The best way to support us is by sharing our tool with others! You can also support us by clicking on our ads or contacting us about sponsorship opportunities.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

Information We Collect

When you use SnapCraft Pro, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Website URLs you submit for screenshot capture
  • IP address and browser information for analytics
  • Usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve user experience
  • Monitor usage and analyze trends
  • Prevent abuse of our service

Data Retention

We do not permanently store the screenshots you generate. They are temporarily processed and then served directly to your browser. Logs containing URLs and IP addresses are retained for up to 30 days for security and analytics purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics for understanding usage patterns
  • Google AdSense for displaying advertisements

Cookies

  • Provide analytics data
  • Support advertising services
