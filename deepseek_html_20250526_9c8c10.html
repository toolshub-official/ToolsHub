Frequently Asked Questions

How many characters can I convert at once?

Our free version allows you to convert up to 1000 characters at a time. For longer texts, you can split your content into multiple parts or consider our premium plans for higher limits.

What audio formats are supported?

Currently, we support MP3 format for downloads, which is compatible with all major devices and platforms. The audio quality is 44.1kHz stereo for optimal listening experience.

Is there a limit to how many conversions I can do?

Our free version allows up to 20 conversions per day. If you need more, we offer premium plans with unlimited conversions and additional features.

Can I use the generated audio for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use the audio files for commercial purposes like videos, presentations, and podcasts. However, you may not resell or redistribute the audio files as standalone products.
