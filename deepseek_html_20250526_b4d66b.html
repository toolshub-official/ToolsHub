Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry. Lorem Ipsum has been the industry's standard dummy text ever since the 1500s, when an unknown printer took a galley of type and scrambled it to make a type specimen book.

Why use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text? +

Using Lorem Ipsum allows designers and developers to focus on the visual and structural aspects of a project without being distracted by readable content. It provides a realistic distribution of letters and word lengths without conveying meaning.

Is this generator free to use? +

Yes, our Lorem Ipsum generator is completely free to use with no limitations. You can generate as much text as you need for both personal and commercial projects.

Can I customize the generated text? +
