Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PDF to Text converter

How does the PDF to Text converter work?

Our PDF to Text converter uses advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology to extract text from your PDF documents. For native PDFs with selectable text, it directly extracts the text content. For scanned documents or image-based PDFs, it uses Google's Document AI to recognize and extract text from the images.

Is there a file size limit for conversion?

Yes, the current file size limit is 10MB per document. This ensures fast processing times and good performance for all users. For larger documents, we recommend splitting them into smaller parts before uploading.

How accurate is the text extraction?

For native PDFs with selectable text, the accuracy is nearly 100%. For scanned documents, the accuracy depends on the quality of the scan but typically ranges from 95-99% for clear, high-quality scans. Our system automatically corrects common OCR errors and maintains the original formatting as much as possible.

Are my documents stored after conversion?

No, we do not store your documents permanently. Your files are temporarily stored during the conversion process (typically less than 1 hour) and then automatically deleted from our servers. The extracted text is only available in your browser and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save it.

What types of PDFs can be converted?

Our converter works with all standard PDF files including:

  • Native PDFs with selectable text
  • Scanned document PDFs (image-based)
  • Password-protected PDFs (if you provide the password)
  • Multi-page documents
  • PDFs containing tables and formatted text

We support PDFs created by all major PDF generators including Adobe Acrobat, Microsoft Office, and various scanning apps.

Is there a limit to how many files I can convert?

For free users, we allow up to 10 conversions per hour. If you need higher limits, you can upgrade to our premium plan which offers unlimited conversions and additional features.
