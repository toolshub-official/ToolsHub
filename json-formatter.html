About JSON Formatter Pro

JSON Formatter Pro is a free online tool created by developers for developers. Our mission is to provide the most efficient and user-friendly JSON formatting solution that works entirely in your browser.

Founded in 2022, we've helped over 500,000 developers format, validate, and beautify their JSON data. Our tool is used by professionals at companies like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my JSON data safe?

Absolutely! Our tool works 100% in your browser. Your JSON never leaves your computer and is not stored or transmitted to any servers.

What's the difference between formatting and minifying JSON?

Formatting (beautifying) adds proper indentation and line breaks to make JSON human-readable. Minifying removes all unnecessary whitespace to make the JSON as compact as possible.

Can I use this tool offline?

Yes! You can download this webpage and use it offline. All functionality works without an internet connection once loaded.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please use our Contact Us form to send us your feedback. We appreciate all suggestions!
