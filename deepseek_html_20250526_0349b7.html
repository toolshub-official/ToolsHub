Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about password security

How does this password generator work?

Our password generator uses cryptographically secure random number generation to create strong, unpredictable passwords. You can customize the length and character types to meet your specific requirements.

Are the passwords stored or transmitted anywhere?

No, all password generation happens locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or log any passwords you generate. Your security and privacy are our top priorities.

What makes a password strong?

A strong password is long (at least 12 characters), includes a mix of character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols), doesn't contain dictionary words or personal information, and is unique to each account.

How often should I change my passwords?

Current security best practices recommend changing passwords only when there's reason to believe they may have been compromised. Instead of frequent changes, focus on using strong, unique passwords for each account.
